Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Toastmaster Timer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Zoom app.

2. Information We Collect

Toastmaster Timer is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Storage Data: The app stores timing rules, agenda items, and reports locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Zoom Participant Information: When you use the app in a Zoom meeting, we may access participant names to help you select speakers. This information is only used within the app and is not transmitted to external servers.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, tracking, or usage statistics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide timer functionality for Toastmasters meetings
  • Manage your agenda and speaker lists
  • Generate timing reports
  • Control virtual backgrounds in Zoom meetings (when you grant permission)

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not maintain any servers or databases that store your information. Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

5. Virtual Backgrounds

When you use the virtual background feature, the app changes your Zoom virtual background to indicate timing phases (green, yellow, red). This requires permission to control your virtual background, which you grant when installing the app. Background images are hosted on our servers but contain no personal information.

6. Third-Party Services

Toastmaster Timer integrates with Zoom's platform. Your use of Zoom is subject to Zoom's Privacy Policy. We do not share your information with any other third-party services.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device. We recommend using standard device security practices such as screen locks and keeping your Zoom client updated.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data at any time through the app
  • Delete your data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Uninstall the app at any time, which will remove all associated permissions

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
