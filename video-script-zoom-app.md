# Toastmaster Timer - Zoom App Introduction Video Script

**Target length:** 2:30 - 3:00 minutes
**Format:** Screen recording + AI presenter narration
**Audience:** Toastmasters club members who run meetings on Zoom

---

## SCENE 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:15)

**Visual:** Quick montage of pain points — a person juggling a phone stopwatch while on Zoom, manually switching virtual backgrounds, fumbling between a spreadsheet and Zoom, looking stressed.

**Narration:**
> "If you've ever been the Timer in a Toastmasters meeting on Zoom, you know the struggle — juggling a stopwatch, manually switching backgrounds for green, yellow, and red, and somehow keeping track of every speaker's time. There has to be a better way."

---

## SCENE 2: Introduce the Product (0:15 - 0:30)

**Visual:** Zoom meeting opens. The Toastmaster Timer app appears in the Zoom sidebar. Show the app logo and the Live tab with the clean UI.

**Narration:**
> "Meet Toastmaster Timer — a Zoom app that runs right inside your meeting. It handles timing, background colors, agenda tracking, and reports — all automatically."

---

## SCENE 3: Starting a Speech - Live Tab (0:30 - 1:10)

### 3a: Set Up a Speaker (0:30 - 0:45)

**Visual:** Show the Live tab. Click on the "Role" dropdown and select "Standard Speech". The timing rules text updates to show "Green: 5:00, Yellow: 6:00, Red: 7:00". Type a speaker name in "Speaker Name" — show the Zoom participant autocomplete suggesting names from the meeting.

**Narration:**
> "Select a role — like Standard Speech — and the app loads the correct Toastmasters timing rules automatically. Start typing a name, and it suggests participants already in your Zoom meeting."

### 3b: Timer in Action (0:45 - 1:10)

**Visual:** Click the green "START" button. The timer starts counting up in large MM:SS format. Show a time-lapse of the background changing colors:
- Blue background at 0:00
- Green background at 5:00
- Yellow background at 6:00
- Red background at 7:00

Then show the toggle between "Timer Card" mode (full background overlay) and "Timer + Camera" mode (face visible with timer). Click the "Reveal Face" eye icon to toggle face visibility in Card mode.

**Narration:**
> "Hit Start, and the timer counts up. Your Zoom virtual background changes automatically — blue while waiting, green when you're in the sweet spot, yellow as a warning, and red when time is up. Everyone in the meeting sees it instantly."
>
> "Choose Timer Card mode for a full-screen color display, or Timer + Camera mode to keep your face visible alongside the timer."

---

## SCENE 4: Finishing & Auto-Advance (1:10 - 1:25)

**Visual:** Click the blue "FINISH" button. Show a brief animation or highlight indicating the speech is saved. The app automatically loads the next speaker from the agenda — the name and role fields update on their own.

**Narration:**
> "When the speech ends, hit Finish. The app records the duration, checks if the speaker qualified, and automatically loads the next person from your agenda. No fumbling between tabs."

---

## SCENE 5: Agenda Management (1:25 - 1:55)

### 5a: Import an Agenda (1:25 - 1:40)

**Visual:** Switch to the "AGENDA" tab. Click the blue "Import Text" button. Show the import modal with the two tabs: "EasySpeak Format" and "Simple Format". Paste a sample EasySpeak meeting export into the textarea. Click "Import". The agenda populates with multiple speakers, each with their roles auto-detected (e.g., "1st Speaker - Standard Speech", "General Evaluator - General Evaluation").

**Narration:**
> "Before the meeting, set up your agenda in seconds. Paste your meeting details from EasySpeak — the official Toastmasters planning tool — and the app auto-detects every speaker and their role. You can also use the simple format: just type names and roles, one per line."

### 5b: Manage the Agenda (1:40 - 1:55)

**Visual:** Show drag-and-drop reordering of speakers (grab the grip icon, move a speaker up). Click the pencil icon to edit a speaker's role. Click on a speaker to load them into the Live tab. Show the green checkmark on completed speakers.

**Narration:**
> "Drag to reorder, edit roles, or click any speaker to load them into the timer. Completed speakers get a checkmark so you always know where you are in the meeting."

---

## SCENE 6: Reports (1:55 - 2:15)

**Visual:** Switch to the "REPORT" tab. Show a table with several completed speeches. Columns visible: Name, Role, Time (in MM:SS), Status (colored dot + label), Over time, and Comments. Highlight a row that shows a red status with "Yes" in the overtime column and a comment like "Passed red by 30 seconds (Disqualified)". Click the blue "Copy Report to Clipboard" button — show the "Copied!" confirmation.

**Narration:**
> "After the meeting, your report is ready. Every speech is logged with its duration, status color, and whether the speaker qualified. The app even generates comments automatically — like how much time was left or if someone went over. Copy the full report to your clipboard and paste it straight into a spreadsheet or your club's chat."

---

## SCENE 7: Timing Rules Customization (2:15 - 2:30)

**Visual:** Open the Edit Rules modal (gear/settings icon). Show the list of pre-configured roles with their timing values. Edit one rule — change the Green value for "Standard Speech". Show adding a new custom role with the "Add role" button. Click "Save Changes".

**Narration:**
> "Every role comes with standard Toastmasters timing rules built in — but you can customize them all. Adjust thresholds, set grace periods before disqualification, or create entirely new roles for your club's unique needs."

---

## SCENE 8: Closing & CTA (2:30 - 2:50)

**Visual:** Pull back to show the full Zoom meeting with the Timer app in the sidebar, backgrounds changing smoothly. Fade to the app logo and Zoom Marketplace link.

**Narration:**
> "Toastmaster Timer takes the stress out of the Timer role — so you can focus on supporting your fellow speakers. Install it free from the Zoom Marketplace and try it at your next meeting."

**On Screen:**
- App logo: Toastmaster Timer
- URL: marketplace.zoom.us (Zoom Marketplace link)
- "Also available as a web app at timer.simple-tech.app"

## Production Notes

### Recommended AI Tool Workflow

| Step | Tool | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| 1. Screen Recording | **FocuSee** or **OBS Studio** | Record the actual Zoom app in action with smart zoom effects |
| 2. AI Presenter | **Synthesia** or **HeyGen** | Generate avatar narration clips from the script above |
| 3. Pain Point Montage | **Kling AI** or **Veo 3** | Generate the opening "struggle" scenes (Scene 1) |
| 4. Editing & Assembly | **Descript** or **CapCut** | Stitch screen recordings with presenter clips, add captions |
| 5. Background Music | **CapCut** or **Descript** built-in library | Add subtle background music |

### Screen Recording Tips
- Use a clean Zoom meeting with 3-5 fake participants for the demo
- Pre-load an agenda with realistic Toastmasters names and roles
- For the color transition time-lapse (Scene 3b), speed up the recording 8-10x
- Make sure your Zoom window + sidebar are clearly visible at 1080p
- Record each scene separately for easier editing

### Visual Style Suggestions
- Clean, minimal — let the product UI speak for itself
- Use subtle zoom-ins to highlight key buttons and features as they're mentioned
- Add brief text callouts for key features (e.g., "Auto Background Change", "One-Click Import")
- Use transitions between scenes (simple fade or slide)
- Consistent lower-third captions for accessibility

### Aspect Ratio Options
- **16:9 (landscape)** — best for YouTube, website embed
- **9:16 (portrait)** — for Instagram Reels, TikTok, Shorts (crop the key UI areas)
- Record in 16:9, then use CapCut's "Smart Trim" to create vertical cuts
