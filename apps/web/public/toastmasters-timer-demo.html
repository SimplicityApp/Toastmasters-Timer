Toastmasters Timer – Full Product Demo

This demo walks you through everything the Toastmasters Timer app can do — from timing your first speech to managing a full meeting agenda. Whether you use the browser version or the Zoom app, this video shows you how it works in practice.

What You'll See in This Demo

  • Green / Yellow / Red timing signals — automatic color changes that tell the speaker exactly where they stand in their allotted time.
  • Speech type presets — pre-loaded timing rules for Standard Speeches, Table Topics, Evaluations, and more. No manual configuration needed.
  • Zoom virtual backgrounds — when running inside Zoom, the app changes your virtual background color so every participant sees the signal instantly.
  • Agenda management — add speakers before the meeting, step through them in order, and generate a timing report at the end.
  • Quick import — paste a list of meeting roles and the app loads them into the agenda automatically.

Try It Yourself

The Toastmasters Timer is completely free — no account or sign-up required. Open it in any modern browser and start timing speeches immediately.
